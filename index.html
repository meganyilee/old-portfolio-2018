About Me

Megan is currently a student at UC Berkeley pursuing degrees in Computer Science and Media Studies. She is especially interested in digital media and is curious about augmented reality, the design and implementation of systems, and artificial intelligence.

For an ongoing 5 years, Megan has spent time working with kids and teens, teaching various digital media courses, math, and communications. At university, she is currently the marketing head of a UC Berkeley student-run publication, Caliber Magazine.

Megan is a Bay Area native, and loves to brunch and explore different beaches in her free time.
